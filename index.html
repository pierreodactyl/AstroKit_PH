Learning Modules

Interactive, student-friendly modules built for DepEd-aligned lessons and STEM clubs.

Module 1: The Hydrogen Line

Understanding the 21 cm spectral line from neutral hydrogen.

Module 2: Radio Astronomy Basics

How radio telescopes work and what they detect.

Module 3: Build Your Own Radio Telescope

Simple DIY kits using RTL-SDR, patch antennas, and LNAs.

Module 4: Mapping the Radio Sky

How students can create hydrogen intensity maps.

Module 5: Astronomy in the Philippines

A look at PhilSA, PAGASA Astronomy, and local research.
