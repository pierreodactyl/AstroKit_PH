Open-Source Planetarium Tools

Essential software for astronomy visualization, data analysis, and mission tracking. All tools listed below are free to use.

Stellarium

VISUALIZATION

A realistic 3D sky for your computer. It shows a realistic sky in 3D, just like you would see with the naked eye, binoculars or a telescope. Essential for planning observations.

  • Download / Access

Celestia

3D SIMULATION

Unlike planetarium software, Celestia allows you to travel throughout the solar system and beyond. View the galaxy from any perspective in 3D space.

  • Download

NASA Radio JOVE

DATA ANALYSIS

Software for viewing and analyzing radio data from Jupiter and the Sun. Perfect for students building the AstroKit dipole antenna to detect solar bursts.

  • Access Archive

NASA DSN Now

LIVE TELEMETRY

Real-time status of the Deep Space Network. See which antennas are currently talking to Voyager, Curiosity, and other spacecraft right now.

  • Launch Live Feed

Zadig (RTL-SDR)

DRIVER UTILITY

Required tool to install the correct USB drivers for your AstroKit RTL-SDR dongle. Without this, your computer will not recognize the radio telescope.

  • Download Zadig

SDR# (Sharp)

SPECTRUM ANALYZER

The standard software for listening to radio signals. Visualizes the "Waterfall" of frequency data. Used in Module 2 for detecting the Hydrogen Line.

  • Download SDR#
